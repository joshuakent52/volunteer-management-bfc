Bingham Family Free Clinic
Volunteer & Student
Confidentiality Agreement
Required for all volunteers, students, interns, and trainees
Purpose

The mission of Bingham Family Free Clinic ("the Clinic") is to provide compassionate, charitable healthcare while maintaining the highest standards of privacy, professionalism, and legal compliance. As a condition of participation, all volunteers, students, interns, and trainees must agree to protect the confidentiality of patient information and Clinic information in accordance with applicable laws and policies.

Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:

  • Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA
  • Patient names, addresses, contact information, and identifiers
  • Medical, mental health, and treatment records
  • Financial, eligibility, or insurance-related information
  • Clinic operational, business, donor, and internal information
  • Information accessed verbally, visually, electronically, or in writing

This obligation applies regardless of format (paper, electronic, verbal).

Legal and Regulatory Compliance

I acknowledge that Bingham Family Free Clinic is:

  • A nonprofit charitable healthcare provider
  • Deemed under the Federal Tort Claims Act (FTCA)
  • Subject to HIPAA, the Federally Supported Health Centers Assistance Act (FSHCAA), and applicable Utah state laws

Covered volunteers and students may be considered federal employees for purposes of medical malpractice claims under FTCA when acting within the scope of approved duties.

Confidentiality Obligations

I agree to:

  1. Access patient or Clinic information only as necessary to perform my assigned duties.
  2. Not use or disclose confidential information for personal, educational, or unauthorized purposes.
  3. Discuss patient information only with authorized Clinic personnel and only in appropriate settings.
  4. Protect login credentials and not share passwords or system access.
  5. Secure physical and electronic records at all times.
  6. Immediately report any suspected or actual privacy breach to the Clinic's Policy and Procedure Officer.
  7. Comply with all Clinic policies, training requirements, and supervision protocols.
Prohibited Conduct

I understand that the following actions are strictly prohibited:

  • Accessing records of patients without a legitimate role in their care
  • Discussing patient information in public areas or on social media
  • Removing Clinic records or devices without authorization
  • Photographing, recording, or copying patient information without permission
  • Using patient stories or data for coursework, presentations, or research without written authorization and Clinic approval
Social Media and Public Disclosure

I agree not to post, share, or discuss any patient-related or confidential Clinic information on social media, websites, or public forums, even if patient names are omitted.

Term of Confidentiality

I understand that my obligation to protect confidential information continues indefinitely, including after my volunteer or student role ends.

Consequences of Violation

I acknowledge that violations of this Agreement may result in:

  • Immediate termination of volunteer or student privileges
  • Loss of FTCA coverage
  • Disciplinary action by educational institutions or licensing bodies
  • Civil or criminal penalties under federal or state law
